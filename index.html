Sihan Wei (魏思涵)

Hi! I am a second-year CS master student at University of Minnesota - Twin Cities, under the supervision of Prof. Ju Sun.

My research interests lie primarily in the intersection of machine learning and optimization, both in theory and practice. Currently, I am working on the performance and stability of faster higher-order methods (tensor methods) for both convex and nonconvex optimization, mainly inspired by the work of Prof. Yurii Nesterov.

Before I came to UMN, I obtained my bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering at Wuhan University.
